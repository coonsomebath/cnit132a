About

This website was constructed to host the portfolio of assignments for CNIT 132A: Advanced HTML & CSS from City College of San Francisco under the guidance of Professor Da Silva.

This site was built using HTML, CSS, JavaScript & discretionary standalone Vue3 components. The decision not to create a Single Page Application was influenced by the desire to streamline the Professor's workflow.

The author aspires to solve interesting problems that has the potential to make the lives of people just a little less burdensome. He is driven by curiosity and is in absolute awe of biological & ecological diversity and processes.

In true San Francisco fashion, the author believes in freedom of expression and the right of people to be who they are. He strives to be respectful, empathetic, and helpful. The author believes in abundance because everybody do not want or prioritize the same things in life. He values diversity of thought & ideas and is always willing to work with people from all stratas in a positive and constructive environment.
